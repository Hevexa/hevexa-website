Hevexa is a software company based in Huber Heights, Ohio. We're not chasing scale — we're building a small number of things well, starting with Braid.

Why Hevexa exists

Built by people who'd use it

Most apps for couples are either bloated with features nobody opens, or so thin they never earn a place on the home screen. Hevexa exists to sit in between — small, well-made software, shaped by actually using the thing every day rather than chasing a roadmap.

Braid is the first product to come out of that approach, and for now it's the only one — every hour we have goes into making it better rather than spreading thin across new ideas.
